The Life of Albert Einstein

Albert Einstein

Theoretical Physicist

Albert Einstein was a German-born physicist who developed the theory of relativity, one of the two pillars of modern physics.

Albert Einstein was born on March 14, 1879, in Ulm, Germany. He is best known for his mass-energy equivalence formula, E = mcÂ², and his contributions to the development of quantum mechanics.

Einstein received the Nobel Prize in Physics in 1921 for his explanation of the photoelectric effect. His work has had a profound impact on our understanding of the universe.
